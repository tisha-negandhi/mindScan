Instructions for the Test

Welcome to the Attention Monitoring Game! This game is designed to help you stay focused on the screen. Follow the instructions below and challenge yourself to improve your attention span.

  • The game uses face detection technology to monitor your attention.
  • Your eye movements are crucial. Ensure both eyes are visible for accurate tracking.
  • Receive real-time feedback on your performance through on-screen messages.
  • Watch out for warnings if your attention drops. An alert will sound if needed.
  • Successfully focus on the camera for a total of 200 seconds. Failing to do so continuously for 100 seconds may indicate potential ADHD.
